About Us

Tanushree Infra & Interiors is a design-driven firm committed to redefining interior and infrastructure spaces. We specialize in residential and commercial interiors, creating environments that are both aesthetically beautiful and functionally efficient.

With a passionate team of designers and craftsmen, we focus on delivering high-quality workmanship using premium materials and innovative techniques. Whether it's a cozy home or a sprawling office, we ensure every project reflects the client's vision and personality.

Our Mission: To provide bespoke and cost-effective solutions that elevate lifestyles and inspire modern living.

Our Vision: To be a trusted name in interior and infrastructure excellence across India.
